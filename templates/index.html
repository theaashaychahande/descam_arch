Why Professional Analysis Matters

In the modern threat landscape, hash-based detection is obsolete. Advanced attackers use polymorphic code that changes its signature every second. Automated antivirus often fails to detect "Zero-Day" threats because they have no known signature.

Professional manual and sandbox analysis at DESCAM goes deeper. We don't just look at what the file is; we look at what it does. By detonating threats in an isolated kernel, we reveal the hidden patterns of ransomware, spyware, and advanced persistent threats (APTs) before they touch your network.
